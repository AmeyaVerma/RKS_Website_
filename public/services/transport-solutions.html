Transport Solutions

RKS Global Logistics provides comprehensive transport and haulage solutions across India, connecting ports, airports, warehouses, factories, and distribution centres with speed and reliability. Our transportation services cover the full spectrum of freight requirements — from small LTL consignments to multi-trailer FTL moves and full container haulage on port-to-door corridors.

We operate and manage a fleet of GPS-tracked vehicles including container trailers, flatbed trucks, closed body vehicles, and specialised carriers. All vehicles are maintained to the highest roadworthiness standards and operated by trained and verified drivers with deep knowledge of key trade corridors and port access routes across the country.

Our transport management system (TMS) provides real-time visibility into all active shipments, allowing clients to monitor their cargo movements through a live dashboard or receive automated status updates via SMS and email at key milestone events throughout the journey.

FTL & LTL Road Freight

Our Full Truck Load (FTL) and Less Than Truck Load (LTL) services cover all major road corridors across India. FTL provides dedicated fleet capacity for large-volume consignments with no co-loading, while LTL consolidation allows smaller shippers to access cost-effective freight solutions without paying for unused vehicle capacity.

Container Haulage

We specialise in first-mile and last-mile container movement between ports, CFS yards, ICDs, and factory or warehouse premises. Our container haulage operations at JNPT, Mundra, Chennai, and other major ports are supported by a dedicated fleet of container trailers operating with AEO-compliant procedures and port authority authorisation.

Multimodal Transportation

We design and execute multimodal transport solutions that combine road, rail, and coastal shipping to optimise cost and transit time for complex origin-to-destination movements. Our logistics engineers evaluate the most efficient routing options based on your cargo characteristics, timeline, and budget requirements.

Door-to-Door Delivery

Our door-to-door delivery service provides complete end-to-end logistics management from the shipper's premises to the consignee's door — including pickup, freight forwarding, customs clearance, and final delivery. This single-vendor solution simplifies your supply chain and provides one point of accountability throughout the shipment lifecycle.
