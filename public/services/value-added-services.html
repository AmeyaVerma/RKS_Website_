Value-Added Services

At RKS Global Logistics, we provide specialized value-added services tailored to enhance supply chain efficiency and transparency.

1. Real-Time Shipment Visibility

Advanced tracking and proactive updates to keep customers informed at every stage.

2. Customized Reporting & MIS

Tailored reports on shipment status, costs, and performance for better decision-making.

3. Cargo Consolidation & Optimization

Smart consolidation planning to reduce costs and improve transit efficiency.

4. Regulatory & Compliance Advisory

Expert guidance on customs regulations, documentation, and international trade compliance.

5. Labeling, Palletization & Packaging Support

Value-added warehouse services to ensure cargo is shipment-ready and compliant.

Short-term and long-term storage solutions with inventory visibility.

Coordinating shipments from multiple vendors into a single optimized shipment.

8. Documentation Accuracy & Audit Support

Minimizing errors and ensuring smooth customs clearance with expert documentation.

Single point of contact for personalized service and faster issue resolution.
